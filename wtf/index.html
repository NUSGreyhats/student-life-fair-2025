Welcome CTF is a beginner-level Capture The Flag competition for NUS students,
aiming to introduce the concept of CTFs in students and pique student's interest
in Cybersecurity!

You can find our challenges from last year here!

This year's Welcome CTF will run for 36 hours, from 16 August 9am - 17 August 9pm.

The CTF is fully online and is totally free to play. Sign up and play the CTF here!
